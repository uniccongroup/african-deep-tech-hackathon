About Omeife Dx: Smartphone-Based AI Diagnostics for Malaria

Advanced deep learning for medical diagnosis

How It Works

Our AI system uses YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art deep learning model, trained on thousands of blood cell images to detect malaria parasites with high accuracy.

Key Features:

  • High Accuracy: 96.4% overall accuracy
  • Fast Analysis: Results in under 1 second
  • High Sensitivity: 97.8% detection rate for infected cells
  • User-Friendly: Simple drag-and-drop interface

Performance Metrics

Model Performance
  • Accuracy: 96.4%
  • Sensitivity: 97.8%
  • Specificity: 95.1%
  • F1-Score: 96.3%
Training Data
  • Total Images: 27,558
  • Training Set: 19,290
  • Validation Set: 5,512
  • Test Set: 2,756

Important Disclaimers

Medical Use Only

This system is designed to assist medical professionals and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis.

Always Consult Healthcare Providers

Final diagnosis and treatment decisions should always be made by qualified healthcare professionals.

Technical Details

  • Model Architecture: YOLOv8 Classification (Nano)
  • Input Size: 224x224 pixels
  • Framework: PyTorch with Ultralytics
  • Training Time: ~3 hours on Tesla T4
  • Model Size: ~6MB
  • Supported Formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, TIFF

For Healthcare Professionals

This tool is designed to assist healthcare workers in resource-limited settings where expert microscopy may not be readily available.

Recommended Use Cases:
  • Initial screening in remote clinics
  • Quality control for microscopy results
  • Training aid for medical students
  • Research and epidemiological studies
Note: This system complements but does not replace traditional microscopy and clinical judgment.
